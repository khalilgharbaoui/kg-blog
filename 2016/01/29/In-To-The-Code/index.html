In To The Code!

There i was, learning PHP, I figured if half the web uses “WordPress” the PHP based platform, it might be the language that needs the most attention.

Looking back i’m so happy i was wrong about that one!!

In the first days of January 2016 a friend of mine told me about a development bootcamp in Amsterdam called Codaisseur, where they teach people to code, “It will only take about 5 to 7 weeks time and you will be able to code” he said. On top of that they would help me with getting a job as a Junior Developer, guide me during the job for 2 years until i reach a senior level.

It sounded really good, but i waited about 2 weeks, then i had to check it out, i send out an e-mail stating that i wanted to participate in the traineeship program.

Mr. W. de Vos of Codaisseur, told me there was only one spot still open and that if i wanted to participate i should at least be able to do the entire Ruby course on the Code Academy website in 48 hours to get a feeling of the intensity of the bootcamp, the first traineeship would already start in 2 days, i only had about 24 hours left to do itüò≥, non the less i didn’t give up, it was now or never!

I huffed and puffed and stayed up all night, pulled it off and sent him the result in the morning.
